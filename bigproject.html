Make Up Must Haves

MOISTURIZER

Great makeup begins with great skincare. Prep your skin with moisturizer and primer.
This will ensure a flawless base that keeps makeup in place all day. Not only that, but it
will also keep your skin healthy after any and all make up products that you use.

CONCEAER AND FOUNDATION

Beginners can eliminate the risk of caked-on foundation by adding concealer to their kit.
L.A. Girl's creaseless formula camouflages imperfections, allowing you to use less
foundation.When paired with Makeup Forever's HD Invisible Cover Foundation, no
one will be able to tell where your skin ends and the makeup begins.These are
a few products to take in consideration, but really any that fits your style is perfect

EYEBROW POMADE AND SPOOLIE BRUSH

Among the most important features on your face the eyebrows are number one. Whether one
wants to admit it or not, the eyebrows make a big difference in ones appearance, which is
why eyebrow pomade and a spoolie brush are must haves. Something to take in consideration,
which I personally consider one of the best pomade and spoolie brush sets, is the dual-sided
Anastasia Beverly Hills tool to brush and pomade. The dual-sided tool helps sculpt perfect
arches and the dipbrow Pomade easy to apply and smudge proof.

EYELINER AND MASCARA

There is really no need to fiddle around with applying false lashes. Although the option will always
be there for anyone who has a personal interest in them, there's is ways to avoid the
application of these falsies. Tag-team Kat Von D Tattoo Liner gives the illusion of a thicker fringe
and with a few swipes of Benefit’s They Are Real Lengthening Volumizing Mascara you will
have dramatic length in seconds

LIPSTICK

No kit is complete without go-to lip colors. Two of the most commonly used lip colors are nude and red.
This is bcause these two hues are universally flattering and versatile, which is just enough to keep you
covered from the office to the dance floor, but of course never be scared to try a new color out. You'll
never know what you can get, even out of those colors that you completely doubt will suit your appearance.

BRUSHES AND TOOLS

In order to put apply your makeup properly you will need to use the right tools. One of many things I would personally suggest using is the Beautyblender. It is perfect for effortlessly blending foundations, blush, and concealer. For precise eyemakeup and dusting on powders reach for Sonia Kashuk's set, reminder these are just suggestions of what I personally believe to be the best products to use, but of course there's many more sets out there in the market. Just make sure to get the one that best suits you and you feel you can work better with. This of course doesn't always mean you have to reach for the higher end products. You'll be surprised how good products can work, even when they're from drug stores.

BLUSH AND HIGHLIGHER

Now, of course we do not want to look completely pale and abnoraml, so to fix that up add healthy color and a subtle glow to your skin with a classic blush and highlighter. Nars and Becca have both created cult favorites that are must-haves for every kit, but like I mentioned before, it's all about finding colors that suit your personal interest, and yes that doesn't always mean they have to be higher end products like the ones I've just listed.

EYESHADOW

To complete the entire look, all you need is some eyeshadow. For beginners this might be scary to try, I can definitely agree with that, but you're never going to learn and get better if you never try. Plus, now in days, there is plenty of make up tutorials in which you can learn which colors go best with which, which tools to use for the best results, and how to blend the products right. I eouldn't recommend getting single eyeshadows, since the majority of the time you're using a variety in one look. My recommendations of a few palettes that can do you great are the Anastasia

Q-TIPS AND MAKEUP WIPES

Even the most skilled makeup artists make mistakes, but don't stress it. Dabbing a Q-tip into lotion is their secret for instantly erasing errors. For those nights when you’re too lazy to wash your face, and yes we all have those moments don't be embarrased, makeup wipes will be your best friend. It's always better to sleep with a clean face then to have a caked on face all night. The more you avoid pimples on your face the happier and better you will feel!
